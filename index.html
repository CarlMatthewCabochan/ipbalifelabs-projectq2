Hexagon Levels

First Stage + Fourth/Hyper Mode

For your first time, you do the first stage of Super Hexagon with a difficulty: "HARD." Upon start, this introduces a rotating Hexagon, with your character being the tiny triangle/arrow. If it's your first time, you get a short tutorial of how to move... Left and Right.

In this game, you have 60 seconds to sucessfully pass each stage. This first stage has its own mechanic. Every 15-25 seconds, it changes into a different shape.

This goes from: Hexagon, Pentagon and Square.

Although, it doesn't have an order, during the "Pentagon" form, there's a half chance it can change back to Hexagon instead of Square.

This doesn't apply to Hexagon or Square since it guarantees will just form to a Pentagon.

Extension/Hyper Stage

After 60 seconds of turning and rotating, you pass the first stage, but, it doesn't end there.

This has a Bonus Stage, which is met after getting through the stage above 60 seconds.

Heads up that this Bonus Stage or being referred as "Hyper Stage" is 2 to 3x harder than the normal stage.

The speed dramatically increases and new obstacles will be thrown at you.

There is also a another bonus stage called "Hyper Bonus Stage." This is when you survived another 60 seconds on the "Hyper Stage."

Second Stage + Fifth/Hyper Mode

After passing the First Stage of Super Hexagon, this time it doesn't have its own mechanics. Meaning no shapeshifts of being Pentagon or Square.

This stage instead focuses on reaction time and precision, its obstacles are much shallower and has less room for error.

And again, after surviving for more than 60 seconds, you get to the Normal Bonus Stage, but this causes the whole map to invert color.

Of course, the speed dramatically increases once getting into that stage, and it also has its own "Hyper Bonus Stage" aswell.

Extension/Hyper Mode

This stage has an invert color of Background White and the obstacles including you flashing reddish to pinkish.

After another 60 seconds of surviving, you get into the "Hyper Bonus Stage" which switches the invertion of the stage.

The obstacles becomes the background color, while the background becomes the obstacles' color.

Third Stage + Hyper Mode/Final Stage

This is the Final Stage of the game named "Hexagonest."

This is the same stage and mechanic of the Second Stage, but this time, its rotation is trickier.

This rotation sometimes fools you, continously rotating and then all of a sudden stops and goes the other way in a swift second.

You will need A LOT of practice to get past this stage.

Bonus Stage/Hyper Hexagonest

This is the "Hyper Hexagonest" stage, and guess what? This is much harder than the normal stage of Hexagonest.
